01 — About

Hi, I'm Yoonseok 👋

I'm pursuing a master's degree at KAIST's Graduate School of Culture Technology, where my research focuses on computer vision within virtual reality environments. I build interactive 3D and WebXR experiences that double as research prototypes. Outside the lab I cook, make coffee, and build things with my hands — a habit that started with LEGO, science kits, and Gundam models as a kid.
